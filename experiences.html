IMMERSIVE ART EXPERIENCES

Thanks to technology's embrace, artists now have the power to craft installations that respond to the presence and touch of viewers, turning art into a participatory experience. With the aid of touch-sensitive screens, motion sensors, and even artificial intelligence, artworks come to life, shifting and changing in harmony with the viewer's interactions. Notable examples of such immersive experiences include the captivating offerings of institutions like WNDR Museum, Superblue Miami, and Experience Van Gogh.

WNDR MUSEUM

"WNDR is a collective of thinkers, artists, poets and designers, working at the intersection of art and technology." "The expansive programs put forth as a collective hub includes our museums, creative studio, and bold collaborations."

WNDR Museum is an innovative fusion of art and technology, showcasing a multisensory experience at its Chicago, San Diego, Boston, and Seattle locations, where artists push the boundaries of experimentation.
