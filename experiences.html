EXPERIENCES

Thanks to technology's embrace, artists now have the power to craft installations that respond to the presence and touch of viewers, turning art into a participatory experience. With the aid of touch-sensitive screens, motion sensors, and even artificial intelligence, artworks come to life, shifting and changing in harmony with the viewer's interactions. Notable examples of such immersive experiences include the captivating offerings of institutions like WNDR Museum, Superblue, and Beyond Van Gogh.

WNDR MUSEUM

WNDR Museum is an innovative fusion of art and technology, showcasing a multisensory experience at its Chicago, San Diego, Boston, and Seattle locations, where artists push the boundaries of experimentation.

SUPERBLUE

Superblue is an organization that supports innovative artists in creating evocative and impactful large-scale immersive art installations. Their first location in Miami is open, with more to come in various cities worldwide. They aim to amplify artists' voices and address important societal issues with a focus on making a lasting impact on how people perceive and understand the world through art experiences.

BEYOND VAN GOGH

Beyond Van Gogh is an immersive art experience and exhibition that has been created to showcase the works of the famous Dutch painter Vincent van Gogh in a unique and interactive way. This experience is designed to take visitors on a journey through the life and art of Vincent van Gogh using a combination of high-quality projections, sound, and interactive elements.
